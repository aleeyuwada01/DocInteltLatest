Engine 3: Target Matrix (Multi-Session Chunking)

Turning $100 into $2,000 in one trade is mathematically suicidal. The Target Matrix proves that by "chunking" the target into separate sprint sessions, you drastically lower the multiplier needed.

The Breaking Points

  • Target Per Session: If you chunk a $2,000 target into 3 sessions, you only need a ~2.7x multiplier per session ($100→$271→$736→$2,000).
  • Fatal Pullback at Peak: As you stack heavy positions in Gold, a pullback of even a few pips wipes out margin. The simulator shows exactly how many pips back triggers a Margin Call.
  • Max Safe Position: The absolute limit of lots you can hold before your broker denies the trade.

Engine 2: Pyramiding (Intra-Trade Stacking)

This measures Intra-Trade Compounding — using floating profit as margin to build a massive position during a single Gold sweep.

  • Initial Capital: The seed money. Must be capital you are detached from.
  • Price Step: Distance Gold must move (e.g., 10 pips = $1.00) before the next stack.
  • Miracle Multiplier: How many times more profit stacking generated vs. a standard hold.

Engine 1: Micro-Sprint (Sequential Compounding)

This measures Sequential Compounding — risking a percentage of a closed winner's balance on the next setup.

  • Trade Window: Your exposure limit. Run sprints, not marathons.
  • Risk / Trade: The velocity of the compound. 15% means losing 15% of your compounded balance.
  • Max Drawdown: The Psychological Pain Metric — largest peak-to-trough drop.

✦ The Gold (XAUUSD) Sweet Spots

1. Pyramiding Sweet Spot (XAUUSD M1/M5)

  • Initial Capital: Very Low ($50–$100).
  • Price Step: 10–15 pips.
  • Stack Levels: 4–6. Stacking 10 times guarantees getting caught in a pullback.
  • Golden Rule: Move SL to BE on older trades the exact second a new stack is placed.

2. Micro-Sprint Sweet Spot (High-Risk Sniper)

  • Trade Window: 8–12 Trades. Past 12 invites the Law of Large Numbers.
  • Win Rate: 60–65%.
  • Risk / Trade: 15–20%. Over 25% is a coin flip.
